# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the defenders who rely on this tool
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Misusing security tools or techniques shared in this project for offensive purposes outside explicitly authorized engagements
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and may take corrective action — including temporary or permanent bans — in response to behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at **hiagokinlevi@protonmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate given the circumstances.
